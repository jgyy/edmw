[OFFICIAL] SINGAPORE COVID-19 Consolidated Info Thread

Reason why i am doing this is because some information is all over the place and i feel consolidating will let everyone have a clearer picture of the full extend of this outbreak here.

All information is compiled using MOH data and also through use of official press releases.
For the tables and graphs presented in this thread, All case data is taken from MOH press releases and annexes.
