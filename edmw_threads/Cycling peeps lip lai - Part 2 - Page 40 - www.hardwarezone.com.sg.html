Wah I jitao lose confident in them at this point. So I took out my N95 mask (with ventilation design) and start to wear it. Then one of the pubor kia said loudly:

Pb kia: Wah bro! Why are you wearing a mask to ride? Later out of breath how? And why your mask got Singapore flag Hahaha?

Me: haha ya lor I love my country mah, almost wanted to draw lightning logo on it one.

Me: You all better wear also lah, it can be transmitted with sweat leh.

Pb kia and others: "dangerous man"  "What if you faint" "

Then knn they ride so fast and dangerously, I just removed my mask and ride on because very hot and my mouth was sweating.

At the rest point, one young man said the corona virus is getting serious and it was just reported that Singapore has kena another few cases. Then he said he is sign on one, SAF might activate him. Then he started to feel panic and was not himself the whole ride.

At the end of the ride, that young man come and ask me:

Young man: bro, now where still got stock for N95 mask ah?

The Pb kia and others just diam diam no words to say or laugh. Can see they are quite concern too. Luckily I stock up alot during the haze period.
