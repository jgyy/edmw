Pear? Easy la. I play until bored. Back to my FF7 SIDE quests again.

How to grow pears: New Horizons. Firstly, you'll need 2000 Nook Miles. Complete the game's many objectives to earn these, then spend them at a Nook Point to buy*a Nook Miles Ticket.

Once you have it, head to Dodo Airlines and you'll be whisked away to another island.These islands tend to be randomly assigned fruit, so keep your eyes peeled for any trees that don't have the same fruit as what you already have.

Once you find a pear tree, give it a shake and eat the pears that fall. This will make your character strong enough to uproot the tree with a single shovel dig.

Great, just head back to the plane and return to your island with your new tree and plant it giving you pears all year round.
