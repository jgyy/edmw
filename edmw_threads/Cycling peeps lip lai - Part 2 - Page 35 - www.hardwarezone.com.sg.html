game goondu wrote:
Usually how many km need to change chain?
Usually use tool(s) to measure. Coz chain design n materials used can make a difference to durability. Your setup of gear ratios, chainline, favourite gears, how often u change chainrings/cogs, tight angles at the rd cage, chain tension, cleanliness of the chain n lube used, user's abuse, degreasing/cleaning method, weather n environment factors, all these can contribute to the wear n tear of the chain. No point use distance to gauge.
