This is for self employed. After u have declared your iras, there will be a NOA. CPF will capture the medisave contribution. It will take awhile for workfare to capture medisave contribution in their system. Once everything in system, u have to contribute your medisave. As for those who mentioned must be by end of May it because of getting the WIS and WSP on July. It not a must by May. As long as you pay all by this year, u still able to get WIS and WSP. E.g. u pay all by end August, u only will get it by end October. It always after 2 months after full payment.

Those who already in giro or still clearing 2018 medisave, do call up workfare to find out about it.
