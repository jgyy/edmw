lbs wrote:
What is the best hand signal to indicate you riding straight away?
I get nervous when i am keeping to the straight lane with a left turn zebra crossing aheading. drivers like to overtake from the right and just zham across ..
For me:

1. Be alert on the traffic around you way before the turn.
2. Speed up to 25-35kph.
3. 20-30m before the turn, I will extend my right hand to tell drivers that I'm going straight.
4. Watch what they do, if they speed up and cut you, then you signal at the vehicles behind him, and pass behind the vehicle that cut you.
Usually they will slow down to let me pass.
