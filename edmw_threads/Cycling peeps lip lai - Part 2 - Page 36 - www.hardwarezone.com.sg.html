Guys, how much does a decent road bike wheel set cost?

Try to get brand new Fulcrum alu wheels.

Don't buy oem carbon wheels thats 600 or less. U pay peanuts u get crap out of it.

Don't buy 2nd hand wheels if u are a noob... Wheels and hubs are the most difficult to inspect especially no sellers will let u open up the hubs on the spot to inspect before buying. Even my experienced fren kena lemons when buying used wheelsets.

If u ask around most cyclists don't service their hubs until 1 yr or even 2 yrs later. During this interval period if the user likes to ride in the rain or use water n degreaser to wash... High chance will get corroded or seepage inside hubs... Esp those fast hubs for racing got minimal sealing.
