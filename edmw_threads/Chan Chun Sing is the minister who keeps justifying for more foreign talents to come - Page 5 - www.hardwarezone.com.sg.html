They know how to point out that easy access to foreign labour results in suppression of wages but at the same time, they seemingly ignore that fact when it comes to PMET jobs. The same reason cannot be applied to PMET? PMET immune to such a factor? Easy access to FT won't make companies lazy when it comes to productivity and innovation?
